About Mento: An AI-Powered Memory Assistant

Mento is an AI-powered memory assistant that captures everything in your life—thoughts, workouts, expenses, ideas—then helps you remember and understand it all through automatic organization and intelligent analysis.

After months of development and testing with our beta community, we're getting close to launch. The core idea is simple: make it effortless to capture anything in your life (thoughts, workouts, expenses, ideas, experiences) and automatically surface patterns and memories you'd otherwise forget. You can speak or type, and the app handles the rest.

Input Methods: Voice, Text, or Structured Templates

The app supports three input methods depending on your needs:

Voice: Tap the microphone and speak naturally. Your words are automatically transcribed using OpenAI's Whisper model.

Text: Standard text input for when you want to type your thoughts.

Quick Note templates: Pre-structured formats for common entry types. The app includes 11 built-in templates (with the ability to create custom ones):

  • Idea Capture - Save ideas with next steps
  • Project Update - Log progress and next actions
  • Expense Tracker - Record spending

  • ...and 6 more built-in templates

Templates work with both voice and text input. The AI adapts to your speaking style and formats entries automatically.

Behind the Scenes: AI-Powered Intelligence

When you speak into Mento, something remarkable happens. Your voice travels through OpenAI's Whisper model—one of the most advanced speech recognition systems ever built. Whisper doesn't just transcribe words; it understands context, handles accents, filters background noise, and even adds proper punctuation. It's trained on 680,000 hours of multilingual data, making it incredibly accurate even in challenging conditions.

But we go further. After transcription, GPT-4 analyzes your entry in real-time, detecting emotional undertones, identifying key topics, and understanding the themes that matter to you. It's not just converting speech to text—it's comprehending the meaning behind your words.

When you use Quick Note templates, the AI adapts to your speaking style. Say "I did 3 sets of 10 pushups today" and it automatically structures it into a proper workout log. Mention "I had an idea about improving our customer onboarding" and it formats it with next action items. The AI understands intent, not just words.

Moments: Historical Entry Surfacing

Moments surfaces entries from the same date in past weeks, months, and years. For example, on January 15th, you might see entries from January 15th last week, last month, and from previous years. This helps you track patterns, see your progress, and understand how you've changed over time.

Available after creating 7 entries.

AI Insights: Automated Analysis

The app uses GPT-4 to analyze your entries and provide:

  • Topic clustering - Identifies recurring themes (work, relationships, health, etc.)
  • Mood tracking - Sentiment analysis over time
  • Periodic summaries - Weekly, monthly, and quarterly narrative summaries
  • Pattern detection - Surfaces correlations between topics and mood

Example summary: "A week of career focus and planning. You wrote about job opportunities 5 times, showing strong anticipation about your future. Key insight: Your confidence is growing as you take concrete steps forward."

All analysis happens automatically. No manual tagging or categorization required.

The AI Engine: Understanding You Over Time

Every entry you create becomes part of your personal knowledge graph. GPT-4 doesn't just read individual entries—it connects the dots across weeks, months, and years of your life.

Here's what happens behind the scenes: When you request insights, the AI performs semantic analysis across your entire journal history. It identifies recurring themes, detects emotional patterns, notices when certain topics correlate with mood changes, and recognizes your personal growth trajectory.

The weekly summaries aren't simple word counts—they're narrative syntheses. The AI understands that you mentioning "feeling stuck" three times in a week is different from mentioning "making progress" three times. It detects sentiment, weighs importance, and crafts summaries that sound like they're written by someone who knows you.

For topic detection, we use advanced embedding models that understand semantic similarity. When you write about "career growth," "job opportunities," and "professional development," the AI recognizes these are all related concepts—even if you never use the same words twice. It clusters your thoughts into meaningful themes without you having to create tags or categories.

The mood analysis goes deeper than positive/negative sentiment. It detects nuanced emotions: anticipation, contentment, frustration, determination, nostalgia. Over time, it learns your personal writing style and emotional expressions, becoming increasingly accurate at understanding your unique voice.

Privacy Architecture

Optional encrypted cloud backup:

  • Back up to a SOC 2 compliant secure database
  • Your data is encrypted on your phone before upload
  • Only you have the key to decrypt it
  • We can't read your entries, ever

The Privacy-AI Balance: How We Do It

Here's the honest truth: powerful AI features require cloud processing. We're completely transparent about this trade-off.

When you use voice transcription or request AI insights, your entries are sent to OpenAI's API over encrypted HTTPS connections. OpenAI processes them in secure, ephemeral compute environments—meaning your data isn't stored on their servers after processing. Per their API policy, your entries are never used to train their models.

For encrypted backup, we use AES-256-GCM encryption—the same bank-level security used by financial institutions. Here's the technical flow: Your phone generates a unique encryption key derived from your device credentials. Before any entry leaves your phone, it's encrypted locally. The encrypted data is then sent to our SOC 2 Type II certified PostgreSQL database. Without your device-specific key, the data is cryptographically useless—even to us.

We architected Mento with a local-first approach. Your SQLite database on-device is the source of truth. The cloud is optional and serves two purposes: encrypted backup for device migration and AI processing for smart features. You can journal completely offline and sync AI features when you're ready.

We don't sell data. We don't show ads. We don't train AI models on your journal. Our business model is simple: you pay $2.99/month for a private space to think and remember. That's it.

Common Questions

Can I use both voice and text?

Yes! Switch between them anytime. Use voice when driving, text when in a meeting.

What are Quick Note templates?

Pre-built formats for common entries (workouts, ideas, expenses, etc.). Just talk or type, and Mento formats it automatically. You can also use templates with freeform voice—just speak naturally about your workout or idea, and AI structures it for you.

Do I need internet to create entries?

Not always. Once signed in, you can create text entries offline. Voice transcription and AI analysis require internet.

What if I lose my phone?

If you enabled encrypted backup, restore on your new phone. Without backup, entries are lost (they only exist on your device).

Can you read my entries?

No. With encryption enabled, your entries are scrambled before leaving your phone. We only see encrypted gibberish.

Does OpenAI train on my entries?

No. Per OpenAI's API policy, data sent via API is not used for training their models.

What if I switch phones?

Sign in on your new phone and restore from encrypted backup (if you set it up).

Is voice transcription accurate?

Yes. Mento uses OpenAI Whisper, one of the most accurate speech recognition systems available.

When do Moments appear?

After creating 7 entries, Moments unlocks and shows you past entries from the same day in previous weeks, months, and years.

What is SOC 2 compliance?

SOC 2 Type II is an industry-standard security certification that proves a company follows strict security, availability, and confidentiality practices. It's audited by independent third parties.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export all your entries at any time.

Can I delete everything?

Yes. You have full control to delete your account and all data.

Technical Details (For Those Who Care)

Data Storage: Local SQLite database on your device, with optional encrypted backup to SOC 2 compliant PostgreSQL.

Encryption: AES-256-GCM encryption (bank-level security) with device-specific encryption keys.

AI Services: OpenAI Whisper for speech-to-text and OpenAI GPT-4 for analysis and summaries.

Backend: Supabase for authentication (Google & Apple Sign-In), with local-first database and optional sync.

Works Offline For: Reading past entries, writing text entries, and viewing insights (if already loaded).

Requires Internet For: Initial sign-in, voice transcription, AI analysis, smart summaries, and optional encrypted backups.
