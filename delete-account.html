Remove your Mento AI account and all associated data

Method 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

In-App Account Deletion
  1. Open the Mento AI app on your device
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom
  3. Scroll down to Account Settings
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Read the warning and confirm deletion
  6. Enter your email to verify
  7. Tap "Permanently Delete" to confirm

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

Email Support Team

If you're unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

  • Email: mentolabsllc@gmail.com
  • Subject: Delete My Account
  • Include: Your registered email address

We'll process your request within 48 hours and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Profile Information: Name, email, profile picture
  • All Journal Entries: Voice recordings, photos, text entries, and metadata
  • AI-Generated Insights: All insights, analyses, and recommendations
  • App Usage Data: Activity logs, preferences, and settings
  • Search History: All search queries and results

What We Retain

For legal and accounting purposes, we may retain the following for a limited time:

  • Subscription Records: Payment history and transaction records (90 days, as required by law)
  • Anonymized Crash Logs: De-identified error reports for app improvement (cannot be linked to you)

All personal and identifiable data is deleted immediately and permanently.
